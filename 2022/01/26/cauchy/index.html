Cauchy-Schwarz Inequality

$$
(\sum_{i=0}^n{a_i^2})(\sum_{i=0}^n{b_i^2})\geq(\sum_{i=0}^{n}{a_ib_i})^2
$$

Ex1

Given $a, b, c\in\mathbb{R}^*, a+b+c=9$, what is the minimum value of $\frac{4}{a}+\frac{9}{b}+\frac{16}{c}$?
